ABOUT US

Welcome to Chartered Institute of Information and Strategy Management

CIISM is an institute that upholds the value of integrity, professionalism, best practice, objectivity, accountability, discipline, leadership, transparency, competency, selflessness as we equipping our members with the highest international standards, knowledge, and capacity building that enhance them to gain competitive advantage in information and strategic management.

Our Mission

At CIISM, we are committed to equipping our members with the highest international standards, knowledge, and capacity building that enhance them to gain competitive advantage in information and strategic management.

Our Vision

We intend to attain the highest international standards in strategic management and information tools excellence by becoming pacesetter, quality bearer, and most respected professional body in information and strategic management.

Our Core Value

Integrity, Professionalism, Best practice, Objectivity, Accountability, Discipline, Leadership, Transparency, Competency, Selflessness

OUR WORK

...

Benefit to Employers

An organization that effectively and efficiently manages its strategy and opportunities is more likely to achieve its business objectives at lower overall costs. Information & strategy management is an inherent part of good management. By supporting your staff through our qualifications, you will benefit from attracting high-caliber professionals by investing in personal development Enhancing business performance, and improving information and strategy taking approaches. Demonstrating the importance you give information and strategy management to suppliers, clients, shareholders and other internal business disciplines such as HR, finance and IT, associating your organization with best information & strategy management practice, receiving discounts on membership, qualifications, training, events, etc

Why Study with Us

Whatever stage you are at in your career, our affordable qualifications programs will help you to develop your Information and strategy management skills and knowledge that can be applied immediately, no matter which sector, organization, or country you work in. Studying with us also demonstrates your credibility with employers. Drawing on theory and practice our qualifications are developed by leading academics and information & strategy management practitioners across the world. This ensures our qualifications are highly practical meeting industry needs with the required academic rigor.
